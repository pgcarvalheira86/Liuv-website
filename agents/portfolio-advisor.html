Portfolio Advisor Agent

Institutional-grade
value analysis, automated.

The analytical core of the LIUV platform. The Portfolio Advisor executes the full 10-Pillar Analysis on individual securities and generates portfolio allocation recommendations grounded in the Buffett/Graham/Damodaran synthesis.

What the Portfolio Advisor Does

Deep value analysis using the 10-pillar framework. DCF valuation, stock screening, and risk-adjusted portfolio allocation. Every recommendation includes full reasoning and explainable rationale so investors can build their own conviction.

Core Capabilities

📈
DCF Intrinsic Value Modeling
Three-stage DCF with high-growth phase (Years 1-5), transition phase (Years 6-10), and terminal value via Gordon Growth Model. Growth rates tied to sector-specific TAM data, calibrated with LIUV's proprietary WACC model.
🔍
10-Pillar Buffett/Graham Screening
Every stock receives a score from 0-100 across ten independently-scored analytical dimensions. The composite LIUV Score synthesizes Buffett/Graham qualitative moat analysis with Damodaran's rigorous quantitative framework.
⚖️
Portfolio Allocation Optimization
Position sizing recommendations grounded in margin of safety principles. Minimum 25% discount to intrinsic value required for a signal, displayed as a safety score (0-100) on every stock card.
🎯
Risk Tolerance Calibration
Pillar weights configurable per user risk profile: Conservative, Balanced, or Aggressive. The agent learns investor preferences and adjusts its analytical lens accordingly across all outputs.

The 10-Pillar Analysis Framework

LIUV's proprietary stock evaluation methodology synthesizes the Buffett/Graham, Damodaran, and Taleb frameworks into ten discrete, independently-scored analytical dimensions.

01
Earnings Quality
Accruals ratio, earnings persistence, restatement history. High-quality earnings score 80+.
02
Intrinsic Value vs. Price
P/IV ratio, Margin of Safety %, and Earnings Power Value vs. current market price.
03
Balance Sheet Fortress
Current ratio, Debt/Equity, Altman Z-Score, and interest coverage analysis.
04
Return on Capital Quality
ROIC vs. WACC spread, 10-year ROIC trend. Consistent ROIC above 15% scores 90+.
05
Competitive Moat
AI narrative analysis plus quantitative margin trend across five moat dimensions.
06
Management Quality
Insider ownership, capital allocation history, CEO tenure, skin-in-the-game scoring.
07
Growth Sustainability
Reinvestment rate x ROIC, TAM penetration, R&D efficiency analysis.
08
Antifragility Profile
Crisis performance scoring (2008, 2020, 2022), revenue diversification, supply chain analysis.
09
Valuation Multiples
EV/EBITDA, P/FCF, EV/Sales vs. sector comps with automatic outlier exclusion.
10
Risk & Tail Profile
CVaR (99%), debt covenants, regulatory risk, ESG controversy scoring.

Investment Methodologies

The Portfolio Advisor integrates three proven investment philosophies into every analysis.

  • Graham Intrinsic Value: Net Current Asset Value screening, Earnings Power Value computation, and margin of safety enforcement
  • Buffett Moat Analysis: Qualitative assessment across network effects, switching costs, cost advantages, intangible assets, and efficient scale
  • Damodaran Three-Stage DCF: Narrative-driven valuation with sector-adjusted multiples, country risk premia, and relative valuation benchmarking
  • Taleb Antifragility: Historical stress-test scoring, fat tail detection via kurtosis analysis, and optionality play identification
  • Scenario Framework: Three forward-looking scenarios (Low, Medium, High) with 10-year projections and margin of safety hurdle verification
🔒 Compliance & Regulatory Alignment
Every output from the Portfolio Advisor passes through a compliance filter layer aligned with MiFID II (EU), SEC/Reg BI (US), and CVM regulations (Brazil). The agent never uses Buy/Hold/Sell language. All analyses conclude with scenario-based projections that help investors build their own conviction through rigorous, transparent analysis.
