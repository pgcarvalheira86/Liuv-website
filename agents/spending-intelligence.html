Spending Intelligence Agent

Transform spending
into investable capital.

The Spending Intelligence Agent transforms raw transaction data into investable capital insights. It applies Taleb's principle that increasing antifragility begins with reducing fragility in personal cash flows.

What the Spending Intelligence Agent Does

Analyzes transaction patterns, identifies savings opportunities, and tracks cash flow to maximize your investable capital. Every dollar saved is a dollar that can compound. The agent turns spending data into a strategic advantage for wealth building.

Core Capabilities

📈
Transaction Pattern Analysis
Discretionary vs. non-discretionary spending classification. The agent identifies recurring patterns, seasonal trends, and anomalies in your spending behavior to surface actionable insights.
💸
Cash Flow Optimization
Monthly investable surplus forecasting. The agent computes your savings rate trend, projects future cash availability, and recommends optimal timing for investment deployments.
📊

Taleb-Inspired Cash Flow Analysis

The Spending Intelligence Agent applies Nassim Taleb's antifragility principles to personal finance.

  • Fragility Detection: Identifies fragile spending patterns such as single-income dependency, high fixed-cost ratios, and excessive recurring commitments
  • Structural Recommendations: Suggests changes that make personal cash flows more resilient to economic shocks and income volatility
  • Savings Rate Optimization: Tracks savings rate trends over time and benchmarks against wealth-building targets aligned with your investment goals
  • Investable Surplus Forecasting: Projects monthly and quarterly investable capital to coordinate with the Portfolio Advisor's deployment recommendations
  • Expense Rationalization: Via negativa approach — identifies spending to eliminate rather than adding complexity to your financial life
🔒 Privacy & Data Security
Transaction data is processed in-session only. No raw transaction data is persisted beyond 30 days without explicit user consent. All bank connections use PSD2-compliant Open Banking APIs (EU), Plaid (US), or direct secure connections (BR). The agent operates within MiFID II, SEC, and CVM regulatory frameworks.
