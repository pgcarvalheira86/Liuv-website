Strategy Agent

Align investments
with long-term goals.

The Strategy Agent operates at the portfolio level, aligning investment decisions with your long-term wealth goals. It integrates Damodaran's scenario analysis methodology with Taleb's barbell construction principles.

What the Strategy Agent Does

Aligns your investments with long-term goals through compound growth modeling and macroeconomic stress testing. The Strategy Agent ensures your portfolio is not just optimized for returns, but structured to survive and thrive across all market conditions.

Core Capabilities

🎯
Goal-Based Investment Planning
Define your wealth targets and time horizons. The agent reverse-engineers the required return rates, contribution schedules, and risk budgets needed to reach each milestone.
📈
Compound Growth Scenarios
Model your wealth trajectory across three pathways: Conservative (6% CAGR), Base (10% CAGR), and Optimistic (15% CAGR). Visualize the power of compounding over 10, 20, and 30-year horizons.
⚠️
Macro Stress-Test Simulations
Portfolios stress-tested against historical scenarios (1987 Black Monday, 2000 dot-com, 2008 GFC, 2020 COVID, 2022 rate shock) and forward-looking scenarios (stagflation, deflation, geopolitical disruption).
⚖️
Rebalancing Recommendations
Drift monitoring with configurable threshold alerts (default: 5% absolute drift). The agent recommends precise rebalancing actions with tax-efficiency considerations built into every suggestion.

Growth Scenario Modeling

Every portfolio is projected across three compound growth pathways, giving investors clarity on the range of possible outcomes.

Conservative
6% CAGR
Capital preservation with steady growth. Weighted toward ultra-safe assets and investment-grade bonds.
Base Case
10% CAGR
Balanced allocation matching historical market returns. The ETF baseline every active strategy must clear.
Optimistic
15% CAGR
Concentrated value positions with high conviction. Requires Buffett-level stock selection and patience.

Taleb Barbell Strategy

The Strategy Agent implements Nassim Taleb's barbell strategy for antifragile portfolio construction.

  • Barbell Module: Automatically computes safe-asset allocation (80-90%) vs. asymmetric-upside allocation (10-20%) based on your antifragility preference setting
  • Macro Stress Testing: Forward-looking scenario analysis including stagflation, deflation, and geopolitical disruption pathways
  • Drift Monitoring: Continuous portfolio drift tracking with configurable threshold alerts and automated rebalancing notifications
  • Tax-Loss Harvesting: Jurisdiction-aware tax optimization suggestions for EU, US, and Brazilian tax codes
  • Scenario Analysis: Damodaran-style narrative-to-number framework applied at the portfolio level, not just individual securities
🔒 Compliance & Regulatory Alignment
The Strategy Agent operates within MiFID II Article 25 suitability rules (EU), SEC/Reg BI guidelines (US), and CVM regulations (Brazil). All growth projections are hypothetical and illustrative only. No guarantee-of-returns language is ever used. Scenario projections help investors understand the range of possible outcomes, not predict specific results.
