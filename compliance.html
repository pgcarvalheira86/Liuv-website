MiFID II European Union

LIUV is designed to comply with the Markets in Financial Instruments Directive II (2014/65/EU), the cornerstone of European financial market regulation. MiFID II sets the framework for transparency, investor protection, and market integrity across the EU.

Transparent Cost Disclosures
All costs and charges associated with LIUV's services are disclosed upfront. Users see exactly what they pay, with no hidden fees. This aligns with MiFID II Article 24 requirements on cost transparency and the obligation to provide fair, clear information.
Suitability Assessments
LIUV's AI agents assess user profiles — including investment objectives, financial situation, risk tolerance, and knowledge level — before generating any analysis. This mirrors the MiFID II suitability requirements under Article 25(2).
Best Execution Standards
When LIUV provides trade analysis or suggestions, the methodology follows best execution principles: evaluating price, cost, speed, likelihood of execution, and order size as prescribed under MiFID II Article 27.
Product Governance & Reporting
LIUV classifies its AI-generated outputs with clear target market definitions and distributes information consistent with MiFID II product governance rules (Article 16(3), 24(2)), including regular review of product suitability.

SEC Guidelines United States

LIUV aligns with SEC regulatory guidance concerning the use of artificial intelligence in investment-related services. We maintain clear boundaries between educational analysis and regulated financial advice.

Informational vs. Personalized Advice
LIUV explicitly distinguishes between informational analysis (market data, stock screening, educational content) and personalized investment advice as defined by the Investment Advisers Act of 1940. LIUV provides the former, not the latter.
AI-Generated Content Disclosure
All analysis and recommendations generated by LIUV are clearly labeled as AI-generated. This complies with SEC guidance on digital engagement practices and predictive analytics (Reg BI alignment), ensuring users understand the nature of the output.
Anti-Fraud Provisions
LIUV does not use misleading performance metrics or guarantee future returns. All historical data references include proper disclaimers consistent with SEC Rule 206(4)-1 (the Marketing Rule) and the anti-fraud provisions of the Securities Act.
Data Privacy & Record-Keeping
User data handling follows SEC cybersecurity risk management guidelines. LIUV maintains appropriate logs of AI recommendations and user interactions for audit purposes, consistent with SEC Rule 204-2 record-keeping requirements.

CVM Regulations Brazil

LIUV is designed with compliance for the Brazilian Securities and Exchange Commission (Comissão de Valores Mobiliários). The CVM regulates the securities market in Brazil and enforces investor protection standards.

Risk Disclosures (CVM Instruction 539)
LIUV embeds comprehensive risk disclosures in all analysis outputs for the Brazilian market. Users are informed about market risk, liquidity risk, and credit risk in compliance with CVM suitability rules.
Investor Protection Measures
The platform enforces proper disclaimers and safeguards for retail investors as required by CVM regulations. This includes clear language that past performance does not guarantee future results, and that AI analysis is educational in nature.
Market Conduct Standards
LIUV does not engage in or facilitate market manipulation, insider trading, or any practice prohibited under CVM Instruction 8/1979. AI agents are designed to analyze only publicly available data and filings.
Cross-Border Considerations
For users operating across Brazilian and international markets, LIUV's Cross-Border Intel agent accounts for CVM foreign investment regulations and provides analysis that respects both local and international regulatory frameworks.

SEBI Regulations India

LIUV is designed with compliance for the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI), the primary regulator of securities markets in India. SEBI enforces stringent investor protection standards and market integrity rules across one of the world's fastest-growing capital markets.

Investment Adviser Regulations (IA Regulations, 2013)
LIUV clearly distinguishes its AI-generated educational analysis from personalized investment advice as defined under SEBI's Investment Advisers Regulations. All outputs are explicitly labeled as informational, ensuring compliance with SEBI's licensing requirements for advisory services.
Risk Disclosures & Suitability
In line with SEBI's investor protection framework, LIUV provides comprehensive risk disclosures for all Indian market analysis. Users are clearly informed about market risk, sectoral risk, and currency risk, consistent with SEBI circular requirements on suitability assessments.
SEBI (PFUTP) Regulations
LIUV adheres to the Prohibition of Fraudulent and Unfair Trade Practices (PFUTP) Regulations, 2003. Our AI agents do not facilitate, suggest, or encourage any manipulative or deceptive trading practices, and all analysis is based solely on publicly available data and filings.
Data Localisation & DPDP Act
LIUV respects India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, and SEBI's data localisation requirements for financial data. User data from Indian markets is handled with appropriate consent mechanisms, purpose limitations, and security safeguards as mandated by Indian data protection law.

Transparent AI Reasoning AI Ethics

Every LIUV recommendation includes full explainable AI rationale. No black boxes. We believe that trust in AI-powered financial tools must be built on transparency, auditability, and clear methodology disclosure.

Explainable AI (XAI) Framework
Every stock analysis, portfolio recommendation, and risk assessment includes a detailed breakdown of the data sources, valuation models (DCF, Graham Number, margin-of-safety calculations), and logic chain behind each conclusion.
No Black-Box Decisions
LIUV rejects opaque ML models for financial decisions. Our AI agents use rule-based reasoning grounded in the Buffett/Graham methodology, combined with transparent scoring systems that users can inspect and understand.
EU AI Act Alignment
LIUV proactively aligns with the EU Artificial Intelligence Act (2024), which classifies AI systems used in financial services as high-risk. We implement risk management, data governance, transparency, and human oversight measures as prescribed.
Bias Monitoring & Model Auditing
LIUV conducts regular audits of AI model outputs to detect and mitigate potential biases — including sector bias, geographic bias, and recency bias — ensuring that analysis remains objective and methodology-driven.

Data Protection & Privacy Global

LIUV handles user data with the highest standards of privacy and security. We comply with global data protection regulations to ensure your financial information remains confidential and secure.

GDPR Compliance (EU)
As a Rotterdam-based company, LIUV fully complies with the General Data Protection Regulation. Users have full rights to access, rectify, port, and delete their data. We process only the minimum data necessary for the service.
LGPD Compliance (Brazil)
LIUV adheres to Brazil's Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados, ensuring lawful processing, purpose limitation, and user consent for Brazilian users. Our data handling meets LGPD requirements under Articles 7 and 11.
Encryption & Security
All data transmitted through LIUV is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). We implement strict access controls, regular security audits, and follow industry best practices for financial data handling.
Third-Party Data Sources
LIUV sources market data exclusively from licensed, reputable third-party providers and public SEC/CVM/SEBI filings. We do not scrape data, and all data provider relationships are disclosed to users.

Our Compliance Commitments

LIUV is committed to maintaining the highest standards of regulatory compliance as we scale across jurisdictions. These principles guide every product decision.

  • Regulation-First Design: Compliance is not an afterthought. Every AI agent feature is reviewed against applicable regulations before deployment.
  • No Personalized Financial Advice: LIUV provides informational and educational analysis only. We never cross the line into regulated financial advice without proper licensing.
  • Continuous Monitoring: We actively track regulatory developments across the EU, US, Brazil, and India. As regulations evolve (particularly around AI in finance), LIUV adapts proactively.
  • User Empowerment: Users always retain full decision-making authority. LIUV equips investors with intelligence; it never makes decisions on their behalf.
  • Transparent Operations: Our methodology, data sources, pricing, and limitations are fully documented and available to every user.
  • Independent Audits: As LIUV grows, we are committed to third-party audits of our AI systems, compliance framework, and data practices.
