Business Development Manager (B2B/Partnerships)

The enterprise API and B2B2C partnerships are LIUV's second major revenue stream. This hire builds relationships with fintech platforms, neobanks, and wealth management firms that want to embed LIUV's intelligence into their own products.

Why This Role Matters

B2B partnerships unlock a revenue stream that scales independently of direct consumer acquisition. This role creates the partnerships that embed LIUV's intelligence across the fintech ecosystem.

Key Responsibilities

  • Identify and pursue B2B2C partnership opportunities with fintech platforms and neobanks
  • Manage the enterprise sales pipeline for the Professional tier and API licensing
  • Build relationships with RIA networks and family office associations
  • Negotiate partnership terms and API licensing agreements
  • Represent LIUV at fintech conferences (Money20/20, Finovate, Web Summit)
  • Support the Series A fundraising process with strategic partnership metrics

Ideal Candidate Profile

  • 5+ years B2B sales or business development in fintech or SaaS
  • Existing network in the European fintech ecosystem
  • Experience with API/platform partnership deals
  • Strong negotiation and relationship skills
  • Understanding of the wealth management industry
  • Bonus: experience at Adyen, Stripe, Plaid, or similar fintech infrastructure companies

What LIUV Offers

  • Equity participation — details discussed individually with each candidate
  • Competitive compensation — discussed during the interview process
  • Remote-first, async culture with flexible schedules
  • Ground-floor opportunity at an AI-native venture studio
  • Direct impact on a product used by investors worldwide
  • Work alongside a mission-driven team democratizing investment intelligence
